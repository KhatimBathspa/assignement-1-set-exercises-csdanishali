Pakistan Biryani Recipe

Biryani is a flavorful and sweet rice dish made with spices, rice, and either chicken, beef, or Mutton. It is a popular dish in South Asian cuisine especially in pakistan and is enjoyed worldwide.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 500g chicken (or beef/Mutton)
  • 1 large onion, sliced
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1/2 cup yogurt
  • 2 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 2 cloves, 2 cardamoms, 1 cinnamon stick
  • 2 tbsp cooking oil
  • Fresh coriander and mint leaves and basil leaves
  • Salt to taste
  • Water as required

Method

  1. Rinse and soak the basmati rice for 30 minutes, then drain.
  2. Heat oil in a pan and the sliced onions until golden brown.
  3. Add ginger-garlic paste and fry for a minute.
  4. Add chopped tomatoes, turmeric, red chili powder, and salt. Cook until soft.
  5. Add chicken (or other protein), yogurt, and whole spices. Cook until meat is tender.
  6. Boil water in a separate pot, add the soaked rice, and cook until almost done. Drain the water.
  7. Layer the half-cooked rice over the cooked meat mixture.
  8. Sprinkle garam masala, coriander, and mint leaves and basil leaves over the top.
  9. Cover the pot and let it cook on low heat (dum) for 20 minutes.
  10. Gently mix before serving hot with raita or salad.
  11. Your amazing biryani is ready Pakistani style
  12. Enjoy your Pakistani style biryani!
